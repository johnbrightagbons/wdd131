Pioneering Players of Sokponba Stake Football Club

Sokponba Stake Football Club actually began in 2015 but officially in 2018 adopted its name with the creation of Sokponba Stake. Over the years, the club has evolved, changing names from Ikpopan Stake FC to Ugbekun FC, before becoming Sokponba Stake Football Club. Our journey reflects our commitment to growth and excellence in football. Join us as we rise together and win together!

The Junior Players of Sokponba Stake Football Club

In 2018, the Junior team of Sokponba Stake Football Club achieved third place in the young men's football competition organized by Stakes in the Benin City Coordinating Council of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints. This accomplishment was led by John Bright Agbons after Francis Edoh stepped down, marking a wonderful experience for the young men.

Senior Players

The Senior team has actively participated in various competitions, including the Benin City Institute League and other local tournaments, showcasing their skills and dedication.
